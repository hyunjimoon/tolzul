## Summary
💭 Theory contribution: Establishes that rational Bayesian learning can explain apparently "behavioral" patterns in entrepreneurial entry and exit, challenging bias-centric explanations.
📐 Practical Value: Provides foundational framework for understanding how pre-entry learning quality affects venture selection and performance patterns.
💸 Evangelist evaluation: Champions systematic learning approaches in entrepreneurship; would strongly support research that builds sophisticated learning tools and decision frameworks.

Computational framework demonstrating that Bayesian learning with pre-entry assessment periods can explain empirical patterns previously attributed to behavioral biases, providing foundation for learning-based interventions.
