## Key Insights

### What Works

1. **Concrete details matter**: All successful SOPs include specific numbers (datasets, awards, publications, countries, downloads)
2. **Technical depth signals seriousness**: Felipe and Thomas spend significant space on methodological details (SDDP, dynamic programming)
3. **Faculty research knowledge**: Thomas and Felipe demonstrate they've read faculty papers and can articulate specific connections
4. **Narrative arc with learning**: Jimin's "grinding effort" and "weekly meetings" show growth process
5. **Balance past and future**: Thomas achieves best balance (35% past / 40% future vision)

### What to Avoid

1. **Vague program praise**: "Outstanding academic leadership" and "supportive atmosphere" are generic
2. **Name-dropping without connection**: Just listing faculty names without explaining research overlap
3. **Pure past accomplishments**: Need to show forward-looking vision
4. **Lack of specificity**: "Real-world problems" without defining which problems
