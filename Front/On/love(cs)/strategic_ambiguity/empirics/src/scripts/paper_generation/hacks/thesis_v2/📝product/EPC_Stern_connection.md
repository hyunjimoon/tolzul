## How EPC Extends Scott Stern's Four Strategic Choices

### Stern's Original Framework (from lecture transcript)

Scott identifies four core entrepreneurial strategies based on two dimensions:
- **Control vs Execution**: Lock in IP vs Move fast
- **Collaborate vs Compete**: Work with incumbents vs Disrupt

This creates four quadrants:
1. **IP Strategy**: Control + Collaborate (Dolby, Qualcomm)
2. **Disruption**: Execute + Compete (Zipcar, Netflix early)
3. **Value Chain**: Execute + Collaborate (Foxconn, suppliers)
4. **Architectural**: Control + Compete (Facebook, platform plays)
