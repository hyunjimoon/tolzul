# 🚀 연구 주제

**“창업가의 약속 설계와 피보팅 전략(Entrepreneurial Promises and Strategic Pivoting)”**

---

## ✳️ [A] 이론10실증90 템플릿

_Theory‑lite, Empirics‑heavy — 실증 중심_

```
Title: The Optimal Ambiguity of Entrepreneurial Promises under Uncertainty:
         Empirical Evidence from Startup Pivoting Decisions

1. INTRODUCTION (10%)
   • Pitch: Why ambiguous promises matter when startups pivot.
   • Research gap: 부족한 empirical evidence linking communication & pivot.
   • Contribution: simple theoretical logic → large empirical test.

2. MINI-MODEL (10%)
   • Parameters: θ (uncertainty), r (resource dependence)
   • Decision: Δ (promise specificity)
   • Objective: maximize expected funding – loss of flexibility
       Δ* = f(θ,r)
   • Theorem summary: 
       θ↑ → Δ*↓ (모호한 약속 증가)
       r↑ → Δ*↑ (명확한 약속 증가)
   • Derived hypotheses:
       H1–H3 from model sign structure.

3. DATA & VARIABLES (25%)
   • Data: PitchBook texts, funding histories, pivot events.
   • Vagueness Index (text embedding), Pivot indicator, Uncertainty metrics.

4. EMPIRICAL MODEL (30%)
   • Logistic / panel regression of Pivot ~ Promise specificity × uncertainty.
   • Endogeneity control (IV: peer-average vagueness).
   • Sensitivity / robustness checks.

5. RESULTS & ANALYSIS (20%)
   • Findings: moderate vagueness → higher pivot success.
   • Visualizations of inverted‑U relation.

6. DISCUSSION & IMPLICATIONS (5%)
   • Theoretical linkage back to model.
   • Implications for entrepreneurial communication strategies.

7. CONCLUSION (≈0–5%)
   • Summary, limitations, future work.
```

> **핵심:** “모델은 가설 생성 도구일 뿐” → ‘데이터가 주연, 이론은 방향성만 제시’.

## ✳️ [B] 이론90실증10 템플릿

_Theory‑heavy, Empirics‑light — 모델 중심_

```
Title: A Theoretical Model of Entrepreneurial Promise Design and Strategic Pivoting

1. INTRODUCTION (10%)
   • Why promises & pivots are central to entrepreneurial dynamics.
   • Gap: lack of formal modeling of communication-based adaptability.
   • Contribution: optimization model integrates uncertainty & pivot cost.

2. MODELING FRAMEWORK (30%)
   • Parameters: θ (market uncertainty), φ (pivot cost), r (resource dependence)
   • Decision variables:
       Δ : promise specificity / ε : pivot threshold
   • Objective:
       Maximize   U = FundingGain(Δ,r) – Loss(Δ,θ) – PivotCost(ε,φ)
   • Boundary conditions, rationality assumptions.

3. THEORETICAL ANALYSIS (35%)
   • Lemma 1: Expected pivot probability ↑ as Δ↓, θ↑.
   • Theorem 1: Lower θ → more specific promises (Δ*↑).
   • Theorem 2: Higher pivot cost φ → fewer pivots (ε*↑).
   • Corollary: Optimal ambiguity mediates between adaptability & credibility.
   • Proof outlines and intuitive discussion.

4. EMPIRICAL ILLUSTRATION / CALIBRATION (10%)
   • Simple descriptive test with 200 startup pitches.
   • Directional consistency check with text-inferred vagueness and pivot events.
   • No strict causal estimation; qualitative validation only.

5. IMPLICATIONS (10%)
   • Theoretical: bridges entrepreneurship & OM under uncertainty.
   • Managerial: guidelines for balancing clarity vs flexibility in promises.

6. CONCLUSION (5%)
   • Summary of analytical insights; future empirical expansion.
```

> **핵심:** “실증은 존재 증명 수준” → ‘모델이 주연, 데이터는 보조적 증명’.

## 📊 두 템플릿 비교표

|항목|**이론10실증90**|**이론90실증10**|
|---|---|---|
|**연구 핵심**|실증적 검증 중심 (데이터→패턴→이론 연결)|이론적 모형 중심 (모델→정리→직관 연결)|
|**이론 역할**|가설 생성 도구 (부호 관계만 제시)|연구 본체 (공식화, 증명, 경계조건 포함)|
|**실증 역할**|주요 분석 (대규모 텍스트・패널 데이터)|보조 검증 (작은 샘플, 방향성 일치 확인)|
|**데이터 비중**|60–70% 본문 (모형 설명은 초반 only)|10–15% (요약 통계·캘리브레이션 수준)|
|**Theorem 사용**|직관적 sign 수준만: “↑→↓”|정식 수학 정리와 증명 (lemma/theorem/corollary)|
|**가설(Hypothesis)**|경험데이터 기반 정식 검증|이론적 파생(derivation) 형태 제시|
|**문체 톤 & 목적**|“우리는 보여준다(we find)”|“우리는 증명한다(we prove)”|
|**적합 저널 예시**|_Management Science (Empirical OM)_, _Organization Science_, _SMJ_|_Operations Research_, _MS (Modeling Track)_, _Manufacturing & Service OM_|
|**논문 길이 구조 비율**|Introduction 10 – Theory 10 – Data/Empirics 70 – Discussion 10|Introduction 10 – Theory/Model 65 – Empirics 10 – Discussion 15|
|**결과 해석 단위**|통계적 유의성, 계수 부호|파라미터 변화의 정성적 Comparative statics|
|**독자 대상**|경험적 연구자, 데이터 과학→OM 교차 관심자|모델링 연구자, 불확실성 최적화 이론가|
