### "Zero to One" Framework

> "Every moment in business happens only once. The next Bill Gates will not build an operating system. The next Larry Page will not build a search engine. If you are copying these guys, you aren't learning from them."

### Core Beliefs from Repository:

1. **Zero to One** (not 1 to n): Creating something new vs. copying
2. **Successful businesses are built on secrets**: What valuable truth do few people agree with you on?
3. **Competition is for losers**: Seek monopoly, not competition
4. **You are not a lottery ticket**: Definite optimism beats indefinite optimism
