### Summary of Each Chapter

1. **â€œTHEY SAY" Starting with What Others Are Saying** This chapter argues that effective writing begins by framing your argument as a response to what others have said ("they say")19191919191919. Rather than starting with your own view in a vacuum, you should summarize the larger conversation your thesis is part of, which gives your writing a clear point and motivation20. 2. **â€œHER POINT ISâ€ The Art of Summarizing** Summarizing is a crucial skill that requires balancing a fair representation of an author's argument with your own agenda22.
